								English words

Enthusiastic - having or showing great excitement and interest.
Resilient - able to recover quickly from difficulties or setbacks.
Ambitious - having a strong desire and determination to achieve success.
Diligent - showing care and effort in one's work or duties.
Comprehend - to understand or grasp the meaning of something.
Ponder - to think about or consider something carefully.
Versatile - able to adapt or be used in different ways or for different purposes.
Convey - to communicate or express a message or feeling.
Enhance - to improve or make something better.
Vital - essential, necessary, or of critical importance.
